Origin Story

In 2012, two health-conscious best friends- Kian and Odette came together to create an organic tea brand that prioritized purity and flavor. They sourced the finest tea leaves from small family-owned farms in India and blended them into unique, delicious combinations with the help of some friends.

The brand gained popularity through word of mouth and social media, and soon became a household name among tea lovers seeking a natural, refreshing beverage.

Our Values

Freshly brewed tea is the best way to experience the flavors of nature's finest ingredients. Our organic herbal teas are hand-crafted using only the finest, ethically sourced ingredients and steeped for lower temperatures to ensure optimal health benefits.

We carefully choose each ingredient in our recipes to ensure they are ecologically sourced, fairly traded, and organically grown. Our goal is to have as small an environmental impact as possible while providing high-quality tea experiences for our customers.

Sourcing

At our tea company, we take pride in our organic process of brewing tea and the art of the delicate infusion of tea leaves. We believe that brewing a perfect cup of tea is not just a process, but an experience that should be savored and enjoyed.

We start by selecting only the finest, organic loose tea leaves from trusted sources around the world. Our tea leaves are carefully selected for their unique flavor profiles, aroma, and appearance.
